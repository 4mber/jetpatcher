A MORE EFFICIENT, MORE COST-EFFECTIVE SOLUTION

JetpatcherTM works closely with local authorities and contractors across the UK to ensure that the HMEP Pothole review for “Right First Time” and responsible Asset Management help meet the growing challenge of pothole and road defect repairs.

Our system integrates smoothly into both planned and reactive maintenance programmes and is ideal for ‘find and fix’ campaigns.

A combination of quality and efficiency, lower carbon emissions and greater cost-effectiveness ensure JetpatcherTM is a key component of road maintenance strategies UK wide.
