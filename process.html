Process



How it Works

The Four Stage Process

0
Miles of rural road repaired
by Our Unique Road Repair System





  • 1

    CLEANED



    Using the high volume blower, the JetpatcherTM easily blows all loose debris and even water from the pothole; cleaning it, and preparing the hole for an effective patch.
  • 2

    PREPARED



    The JetpatcherTM now coats the pothole with an asphalt emulsion which seals the pothole and prevents further damage from occurring, due to moisture.
  • 3

    FILLED



    As the aggregate passes through the nozzle it is coated with emulsion and sprayed into the hole or broken road edge. The JetpatcherTM asphalt mix is sprayed at speeds of up to 100kph, compaction from the bottom up is achieved; denser than rolled asphalt.
  • 4

    SEALED



    The wet top of the repaired area can now be protected from traffic flow, using either dry aggregate sprayed from the JetpatcherTM, or, for inner city work (where loose chip is a potential hazard), sand from optional sand hopper which can be built into the JetpatcherTM unit.
